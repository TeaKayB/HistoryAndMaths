Which channel should I use?

If you have a general question about cross-curricular lesson ideas, book reviews, or teaching strategies, we highly encourage posting directly to the History and Maths in Education Discussion List so the entire community can benefit from the conversation, but bear in mind that this is a public forum.

For non-HaMiEN things, personal, administrative, or other private matters, email is probably best.
